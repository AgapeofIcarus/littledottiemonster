Need a little more

Goal
Met

Calculate your daily goal in ounces, then divide that by 4. Enter that number into the field and, once you've met each mark, click the arrow to the right to help Little Monster. If you clicked too soon, then click the one to the left and it will take you back a step.
Example: My daily goal is 64 ounces per day. My goals will be 16, 32, 48, and 64.
